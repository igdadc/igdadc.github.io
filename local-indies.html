Local Indie Games

This is a simple list of all the local indie steam games we know about. Most made by people who have attended at least 1 IGDA DC event, some are other more Baltimore based devs we know about. If you want to add your game to the list, make sure you come to a meeting at least once and then message Joey, or another admin, on the Discord to be added to this page.
