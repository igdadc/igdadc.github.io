District Arcade 2025


District Arcade returns on October 25th 2025 1:00-5:00 PM at the Martin Luther King Library in DC. The meetup and lineup is still being worked on, however the date and location have been confirmed.

What is District Arcade, you ask? Itâ€™s a free celebration of games and game makers from the DC and DMV area. Play locally-crafted indie games and chat with the devs in this fun, family-friendly in person event. There will be 15-20 local developers showcasing games of different genres and levels of completion.

Event Information
  • Location: Lab - Bottom floor of the MLK Library
  • Address: 901 G St NW, Washington DC, 20001
