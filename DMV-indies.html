DMV Indie Games

This is a simple list of all the indie steam games we know about made by devs from the DMV. Most of these are made by people who have attended at least 1 IGDA DC event, others are more Baltimore based devs. If you want to add your game to the list, make sure you come to a meeting at least once and then message Joey, or another admin, on the Discord to be added to this page.
