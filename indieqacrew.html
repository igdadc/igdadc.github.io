IGDA DC Indie QA Crew

The Indie QA Crew is an initiative run by the board members of IGDA DC to serve both indie developers in our group and aspiring QA professionals by connecting developers who need game testing with volunteer testers. We hope this initiative helps provide developers with more consistent and relevant feedback when they need it for their game projects and provides volunteers with a professional-level experience of game QA work.

How To Join

This Google Doc contains all the information about becoming a tester or submitting a game. The whole process is managed via our Discord so you should join that if you are interested. If you have any questions about the program post them in the #playtesting channel on our Discord or message one of the board members.
