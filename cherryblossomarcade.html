Cherry Blossom Arcade 2025



Cherry Blossom Arcade is a new smaller indie showcase we are starting up this year. It is happening Sunday May 25th 2025 from 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM at the MLK library in D.C. Here is the Meetup page for the event. If you plan on coming please join the Meetup event so we can estimate attendance.

What is Cherry Blossom Arcade, you ask? It’s a free celebration of games and game makers from the DC and DMV area. Play locally-crafted indie games and chat with the devs in this fun, family-friendly in person event. There will be 15-20 local developers showcasing games of different genres and levels of completion. This is intended as a smaller version of District Arcade that takes place earlier in the year.

This year we are also hosting a mini raffle/competition where attendeees recieve a checklist of four games at the beginning of the event. If the attendee plays those four games and recieves a sticker from the devs they can exchange that checklist for a Steam key of a random game, some of which are made by local developers.
