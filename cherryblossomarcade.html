Cherry Blossom Arcade 2026



Cherry Blossom Arcade is an annual Indie showcase run by IGDA DC. It is happening Saturday April 18th 2026 from 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM at the MLK library in D.C. Here is the Meetup page for the event. If you plan on coming please join the Meetup event so we can estimate attendance.

What is Cherry Blossom Arcade, you ask? It’s a free celebration of games and game makers from the DC and DMV area. Play locally-crafted indie games and chat with the devs in this fun, family-friendly in person event. There will be 20-30 local developers showcasing games of different genres and levels of completion. This is intended as a smaller version of District Arcade that takes place earlier in the year.

Event Information
  • Bottom Floor - MLK Library in Washington D.C
  • Address: 9th and G Street, Gallery Place Metro Stop · Washington, DC
  • Date and Time: April 18th 2026 1:00-5:00 PM
